Assalamualaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh
This are the beautiful and divine attributes or names of God in Islam. These names are found in various Hadith (sayings and actions of the Prophet Muhammad) and are commonly recited by Muslims in prayer

Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ peace be upon him) said, “Allah has ninety-nine names,one-hundred minus one, and whoever knows them will go to Paradise.” (Sahih Bukhari 50:894)
